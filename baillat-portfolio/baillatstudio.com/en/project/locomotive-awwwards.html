About

To highlight digital agency Locomotive being named agency of the year for the third time running, Baillat Studio produced an out-of-home campaign and promotional video. The studio created a series of elegant visuals based on the client's aesthetic that highlighted its achievement by positioning repetition as a central element of the design.

For this project, Baillat Studio worked with photographer Mathieu Lévesque, who is represented by Consulat, as well as Hologramme and Homy Studio for the video's sound design. The results were used by Locomotive on their social media channels and website.
