Me     .

MANIFESTO

Design for sustainability, scalability, and efficiency have always been my design philosophy. I constantly push myself to make designs that can be documented, repurposed, and expanded.

I believe making great design is just like creating libraries in coding - the outcome becomes part of the technological evolution, and will be passed on to others, repurposed by others, and spark innovation.

WORKING
HISTORY

I've made designs for startups at different stages, most of them being customer-facing business.

In mid-2020, I joined LEX, a venture-backed fintech startup in NYC. Since then, I've had the privilege to build many things from scratch - brand identity, core product on Web and iOS, company website, and design systems.

Before LEX, I worked at ByteDance and Meituan Bike. I've also done a few freelance projects before I started my professional career.

EDUCATION

Back in college, I studied at NYU on Interactive Media Arts and Computer Science. My focuses were interactive programming, human perception, and visual communication.

I'm confident that my educational background armed me with the skills and methods to deliver thoughtful and well-executed designs in various media forms.
