Our Task

Our task was to create the pre-event Leadership infographic and do Live Scribing at one of the Deloitte’s internal meeting in their offices in Belgium. We had different themes on various topics (health, robotics, Diversity, etc.) we think something like this could work too. However, given it is a complex, ambiguous, world the visual should not be linear with a defined start and endpoint.

Finding a way to connect themes pictures with words was our challenge

The Process

Deloitte’s client wanted to show how the overarching theme above unites the different elements.

Brining in unusual visual representations from the art world that people do not get visual themselves. Se, we used the words volatile, uncertain, complex, ambiguous to spark inspiration for these elements?

Influencing the inner world of I for impact in the external world – breathing through your heart (this could be an excellent centerpiece).
