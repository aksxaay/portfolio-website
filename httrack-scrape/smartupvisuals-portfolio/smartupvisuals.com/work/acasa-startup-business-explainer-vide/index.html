An animated explainer video is what every startup needs! Acasa needed one too. We listened to the founder’s brief and dug into the teams’ values, brand, and product. Acasa is a long-time friend of ours, and we have helped them with many branding, design, and visual communication-related projects in the past. So, getting familiar with their brand requirements was a job half done already. Acasa were an especially fun client because they had native iOS and Android apps, website, blog and all the social media platforms that the “cool kids” are on, so we had a lot to play with!

Play

The process

In general, the more we work with brands on a recurring basis, the more depth is added to the assets we create and the more efficient our delivery becomes. Once our team is familiar with your needs, way of working, and organizational culture, we can skip the small talk and dive straight into delivering the best of the best for you, completely bespoke!

And that is what happened here at acasa (a home management platform designed to create a seamless living). We loved acasa’s brand values:

  • Live and learn.
  • Go the extra mile.
  • Be a champion!

Their values became ours while working together and we hope it reflected in this project. Enjoy the fun and engaging animation we created for Acasa!

PS Huge congratulations to the team for their recent successful exit to Goodlord, we can’t wait to see where this takes the product!
