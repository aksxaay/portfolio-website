TURS

TURS is a soft robotic human body extension that live-tracks and reacts to ultraviolet light radiation. The physical implementation reimagines the integration of machines into the functions of the flesh in the posthumanism era and its enhancement to human sensations.

Soft Robotics, Installation
2020
