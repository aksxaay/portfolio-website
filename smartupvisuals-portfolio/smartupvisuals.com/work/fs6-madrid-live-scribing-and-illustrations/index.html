FS6: Madrid Live Scribing and Illustrations

Client Scaling up Masterclass
Year 2019
Services Live Scribing
Infographics

We hopped on a plane to Madrid and helped the attendees showcase these learnings and experiences through Live Scribing and Illustrations.

Scaling up Masterclass is an interactive masterclass in preparation for South Summit, which took place at Google for Startups, Madrid. This Masterclass offers learnings from the failures and successes of over 400 participant startups and leaders that have explored the hottest ecosystems and successfully scaled up abroad.

We hopped on a plane to Madrid and helped the attendees showcase these learnings and experiences through Live Scribing and Illustrations.

Our team drew illustrations digitally while listening to inspiring sessions in real time. We covered many topics: Startups, Innovation, European Collaboration, Entrepreneurship, Ecosystems, Business Growth, and Collaboration! 

Oh, and if you wonder who designed their pre-event booklets and attendees’ t-shirts?

That was us too 🙂

Our illustrated booklets highlighted the most popular European capitals in the startup space: Berlin, Paris, Vilnius, the Netherlands, Dublin & Lisbon.
