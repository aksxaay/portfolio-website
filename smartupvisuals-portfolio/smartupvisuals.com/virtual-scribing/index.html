What are the types of Live Scribing?

Live Scribing comes in many forms. From the traditional, hand-drawn style, it has evolved into Digital Remote options. Business goals vary. To accommodate our clients, we offer a variety of scribing options, from location-based services such as: handwritten or digital live scribing to remote scribing service – check out he Virtual Scribing.

How does Remote Virtual Scribing work?

Live remote virtual scribing is the most convenient way to capture online events. A Scribe will participate in your digital event on a provided communication platform. During the meeting, he will remain silent and record the outcomes silently. In the end, you will have an event infographic that can be shared. We can also arrange a more interactive session if the Live Remote Scribing process is shown to everyone.
