Privacy

Our commitment to privacy

We are committed to creating websites that gather as little information about visitors as possible. We seek an internet that is open and transparent and that allows for the free expression of ideas — not for furthering the goals of social media companies and search advertising platforms.

We do not use cookies on this site. We do not track individual visitors on this site. We do not spam our followers. And we do not connect our website to Facebook.

Your personal information is worth protecting. It’s important to us that you know how we take steps to guard your privacy on this website and in our interactions with you.

Last updated: November 11, 2021

Our privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our website. We regularly review not only our policy but the policies of third party analytics platforms. By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this privacy policy.

We do not use Google Analytics on this website, which means that Google is not tracking you on our pages. We prefer to use Fathom Analytics, an independent platform, which helps us anonymously understand our visitors. Importantly, Fathom does not use cookies and is GDPR compliant.

We use Campaign Monitor for our email newsletter. Campaign Monitor is a smaller and independent email newsletter provider based in Australia and is GDPR compliant.

Our email newsletters do track subscriber opens and clicks and it is not currently possible to turn off that tracking code. We are examining alternatives. If you are interested, please read our full privacy policy.
